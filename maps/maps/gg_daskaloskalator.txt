23 School "Frederic Joliot-Curie" is a secondary school in the district. "Slatina" in Sofia.


The school was founded in 1957 as an elementary school named "Antim I", and in 1960 adopted its current name with a cartridge great scientist Frederic Joliot-Curie. Originally located on top alternating with a Vocational School. In 1969 it merged with 24 High school of "Hadji Dimitar" and many hadzhidimitrovtsi complete it. In the same year was completed and the new building of the school on the boulevard. "Sitnyakovo" (former "Hristo Kabakchiev"). Director then historian Ephraim Kochev. In 1975, he received the diploma of the region "Vasil Levski" and "Cyril and Methodius".

Modern state 
Currently director of the school Mrs. Nina Chaneva, professor of Mathematics. Deputy directors Krasimira Joncheva and Elena Draganova.

In 2014 the school opened the first "Smart Classroom" in Bulgaria.

There are over 1270 students and more than 25 teachers.
